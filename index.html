Frequently Asked Questions


How does this work?

The app uses your NUSNET credentials and logins to the library portal on your behalf, searches for the past year exam papers for a module, and downloads them to a specified folder on your computer.


Are my credentials safe?

Yes. Even though you enter your credentials into the app, the app does not store your credentials and the login is secure via the HTTPS protocol. The source code is open sourced on Github.

However, you should not download the app from other sources other than this official page as they might not have been created from our source code and might contain malicious intent.
